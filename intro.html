Introduction

Around the world, company investments in Machine Learning have increased significantly, and “91% of top businesses have reported having ongoing investments in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning” (O’Donnellan, 2024).

Machine Learning and Cybersecurity are vital in the modern-day world, and with more frequent cyber-attacks taking place, as shown in Figure 2, and new methods to defend against cybercriminals being investigated, it is more important than ever to understand this complex field. This article investigates the effectiveness of ML in Cybersecurity and how ML is used to defend against Data Mining.

In this research project, there are 3 sections, which will cover various topics:

Machine Learning:

- What is it?
- What are the typical uses?

Cybersecurity:

- What is it and why is it important?
- What is the link between Cybersecurity and Machine Learning?

Data Mining:

- What is it?
- Why does Data Mining pose a threat to Cybersecurity?
- How does Machine Learning defend against Data Mining?
