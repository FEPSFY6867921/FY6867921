Introduction

Around the world, company investments in Machine Learning have increased significantly, and “91% of top businesses have reported having ongoing investments in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning” (O’Donnellan, 2024).

Machine Learning and Cybersecurity are vital in the modern-day, and with new methods to defend against cybercriminals, it has become increasingly important to understand these methods. This is why I have chosen to investigate the effectiveness of Machine Learning in Cybersecurity and find out how it is used to defend against Data Mining.

Machine Learning:

Cybersecurity:

- What is the link between Cybersecurity and Machine Learning?

Data Mining:

- What is it?

- How does Machine Learning defend against Data Mining?
