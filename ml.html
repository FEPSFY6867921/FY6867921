What is Machine Learning?

Machine learning (ML) is a subset of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that consists of algorithms that reflect how humans learn, hence granting this learning capability to technology. Initially, when these programs are written, they are very inaccurate and have many faults, but over a prolonged period of time, the accuracy of the technology improves. (Hosch, 2024)
